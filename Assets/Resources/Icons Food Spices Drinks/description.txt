Icons Food Drinks Spices
by David Baumgart

Richly detailed, hand-painted icons suitable for a strategy game, RPG, or boardgame-like experience. This set is packed with veggies, meats, spices, herbs, and a couple potent potables to round out your feast-filled game!

- 84 icons (so far!)
- all icons are 128x128 pixels
- provided on a transparent background for maximum flexibility

Icons are provided as individual alpha-transparency PNG files.
